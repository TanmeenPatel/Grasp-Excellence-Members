About Us

We started off as a humble company, a result of a merger of 2 companies; Grasp Excellence and Suraiyaa Jaan Quizzes. Both companies had the same motive: to provide quality education to our classmates. They felt the need for something that would help them revise what was learnt in school. Since no one took the steps to make such a company, they set out to do so themselves. With sheer hardword, determination and sincerity, they were able to make a successful company. There was great competition between the 2 companies, and that's when the owners realised: they had the same vision. It would be the best of both worlds if the 2 companies became 1 collective company that served the class. And this website is for that company.
